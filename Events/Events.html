A PROTEST BY THE UNIVERSITY OF MANOUBA

On Thursday, May 2, 2024, starting at noon, a protest was held at the University of Manouba, next to the Carthage Modern Auditorium. This protest is in support of Palestine, demanding condemnation of genocide, and calling for an immediate ceasefire (involving professors, students, administrators, and workers).

A VISIT TO THE KASSAB HOSPITAL

Visiting injured Palestinians at the orthopedic hospital Mohammed Kassab in Manouba and providing donations to aid in the recovery process have brought a sense of comfort and reassurance to those undergoing treatment.

MEET UP FOR DONATIONS

A meet up was organised by the Red Crescent Manouba Comittee in Manouba for people to donate to Palestinians in Gaza. This event was really successful as numerous generous people have donated. The donations have been sent by our government to our brothers and sisters in Gaza.
