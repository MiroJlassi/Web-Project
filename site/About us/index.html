The Tunisian Red Crescent is a non-profit organization.

It was created on October 7, 1956, and officially recognized by government decree on May 6, 1957. As an auxiliary to public authorities, the Tunisian Red Crescent engages in humanitarian activities in accordance with the Geneva Conventions of 19491. Its principles include humanity, impartiality, neutrality, and independence. The organization aims to protect life, health, and human dignity, fostering understanding, cooperation, and lasting peace among all people.
